I am a first-year Machine Learning PhD Student in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology advised by Professor Judy Hoffman.

I am broadly interested in multi-task learning, natural language processing, and never ending learning. Ultimately, I hope to bridge the gap between human and machine learning.

Previously, I was a Research Assistant in the Machine Learning Department at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Professor Barnabás Póczos. I hold a Bachelors degree in Statistics and Machine Learning from Carnegie Mellon University with School of Computer Science and University Honors.
